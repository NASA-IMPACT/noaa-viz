NOAA: ESRL Global Monitoring Laboratory: CH4 PFP

The CH4 PFP data provides methane dry air mole fractions using air samples collected in glass flasks from 28 NOAA GML Carbon Cycle Cooperative Global Air Sampling Network sites across the United States. Samples were analyzed for methane by gas chromatography with flame ionization (GC/FID) detection or by cavity ring-down spectrometer (CRDS).
